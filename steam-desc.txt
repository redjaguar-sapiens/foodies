Adds more food options and culinary decorations to Sapiens.

Added:
- Cooking Pot (used for making below recipes)
- Gruel (1 x hulled wheat + fired bowl + fired cooking pot)
- Beetroot Soup (2 x beetroots + fired bowl + fired cooking pot)
- Pumpkin Soup (1 x pumpkin + fired bowl + fired cooking pot)

Notes:
- All recipes are made at the campfire. Will require Basic Cooking and Pottery research (plus Threshing for gruel)
- Can only use fired bowls and fired cooking pots
- Soups/gruels are categorised as Food Bowls for storage and filters.
- Food Bowls go rotten after 3 days. Similar to Medicine Bowls, which is 2 days

Full details and roadmap on Github: https://github.com/redjaguar-sapiens/foodies
