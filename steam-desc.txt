Adds more food options and culinary decorations to Sapiens.

Added:
- Gruel (1 x hulled wheat + bowl)
- Beetroot Soup (2 x beetroots + bowl)
- Pumpkin Soup (1 x pumpkin + bowl)
- Cooking Pot (decoration only for now)

Notes:
- All food is made at the campfire. Will require Basic Cooking and Pottery research (plus Threshing for Gruel)
- Can use either fired or unfired bowls
- Categorised as Food Bowls for storage and filters.
- Food Bowls don't currently expire/go rotten, but will do in a future version.

Full details and roadmap on Github: https://github.com/redjaguar-sapiens/foodies
